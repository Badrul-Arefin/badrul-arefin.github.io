Hello, Welcome to my personal website! This website was built mainly to describe my research profile to the like minded and interested people. Please go to the respective sections to know more about my research and myself. In About page, I tried to describe my research works in relatively plain english that aimed at people who came from different background than biology. By the way, My name is Badrul Arefin, a postdoctoral fellow at the university of Gothenburg, Sweden. Currently I am working in cancer biology, more specifically in neuroblastoma, a childhood cancer that accounts for 15% of childhood cancer mortality. It is a disease which arises from the sympatatic nervous system. Our aim is to understand the key events that drive the neuroblastoma in mouse and Drosophila models at single cell resolution.
